Welcome to the 2015 Biggest Loser: Hong Edition!

The goal of this challenge is to motivate ourselves to lose weight before Svensica’s wedding in an accountable, healthy and fun way.



Rules:

16 week weight loss* challenge w/ $40 buy-in:

  1. Challenge goes from Feb 16 (Mon) thru June 7 (Sun)
    1. Points are awarded for hitting your final weight loss goal, and for achieving linear weight loss along the way (don’t drop all the weight in the last week or two!!)
    2. Total pot at the end of challenge will be divided proportionally by the number of points accrued by each participant
    3. *Only Daniel may participate with a weight gain goal!
  2. Starting steps:
    1. Weigh yourself the week of Feb 9-15 and submit your starting weight and weight loss goal through the website (must be visually verified by someone - okay to use skype or similar video chat to do so)
    2. NOTE: Your total weight will never be disclosed to any of the participants; only weight deltas will be charted so we can see each other’s progress
  3. Weigh-ins are weekly, and can be submitted at any time during a week (a week goes from Monday thru following Sunday)
    1. Similar clothing (or lack thereof) must be worn during each weigh-in (no cheating by wearing winter parka and holding cat during initial weigh-in)
    2. Weigh-in must be visually verified by a buddy (okay to use skype or similar video chat to do so)
  4. Points are accrued during milestone (MS) weeks:
    1. MS #1: March 9 -15 - if you are ¼ of the way to your weight loss goal, you accrue 5 points
    2. MS #2: April 6 - 12 - if you are ½ of the way to your goal, you accrue 5 points
    3. MS #3: May 4-10 - if you are ¾ of the way to your goal, you accrue 5 points
    4. MS #4 (final): June 1-7 - if you have met your goal, you accrue 10 points
    5. Total possible points: 25
